Creamy Spinach Dip: Great for all occasions

Created by Robbie S. Abrams, provided by her Grandson Charles Abrams

Ingredients

  • 8 oz. Sour Cream
  • 1 cup Mayo
  • 1/2 tsp. celery salt
  • 1.2 tsp. dill weed
  • 1/4 tsp. onion salt
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 3 cups frozen cut leaf spinach, thawed and well-drained
  • 8 oz water chestnuts, finely chopped
  • 3 tbs. chopped red pepper, if desired

Directions

  1. In a medium bowl, combine sour cream, mayo and seasoning.
  2. Stir in onions, spinach, water chestnuts and red pepper.
  3. Cover, refrigerate to blend flavors

Can be served in a hollowed out load of sheep hearders bread.
